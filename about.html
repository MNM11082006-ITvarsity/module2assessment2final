About Pine City Mall

Pine City Mall has been the heart of local shopping, dining, and entertainment since 2002. With over 100 stores, a luxury cinema, and a variety of eateries, we bring you everything you need under one roof.

Our mission is to make your shopping experience seamless, enjoyable, and memorable. Whether you're here to shop, eat, or relax— Pine City Mall is your go-to destination.
